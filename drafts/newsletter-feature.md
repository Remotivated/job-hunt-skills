We built a thing.

It's called **Job Hunt OS** — a free, open-source collection of AI skills, prompts, and guides designed for people searching for remote work.

### Why we built it

Remote roles make up roughly 8-12% of job postings but attract over 40% of applications. That's 4x the competition for a fraction of the opportunities.

Most job search advice ignores this math. "Apply to more jobs" doesn't work when the pond is this small. You need precision — better targets, stronger applications, higher conversion on every opportunity.

We've been thinking about this problem for a while. Our email course (the Remote Job Hunt OS) laid out the philosophy. But philosophy without tools is just advice. So we turned the methodology into something you can actually use.

### What's in it

**7 Claude skills** that work in Claude Code:
- **Resume Builder** — Creates your resume through guided Q&A, not a template fill
- **Resume Auditor** — Gives genuinely critical feedback (we engineered it to counteract AI's tendency to tell you everything looks great)
- **Resume Tailor** — Customizes your resume for a specific role without inventing details
- **Company Radar** — Vets a company's remote culture before you waste time applying
- **Interview Coach** — Generates prep briefs with questions drawn from YOUR actual experience
- **Proof Asset Creator** — Builds case studies and portfolio pieces
- **LinkedIn Optimizer** — Audits and rewrites your profile sections

**6 standalone prompts** that work in any AI — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, whatever you use. Copy, paste, go.

**9 methodology guides** covering everything from the competition math to negotiation scripts to sustainable search habits.

### The one we're most proud of

The Resume Auditor. Most AI tools will tell you your resume is "impressive" and suggest minor tweaks. That's not useful — it's sycophancy.

We specifically designed this skill to lead with what needs improvement. It rates every bullet (Strong / Needs Work / Weak), identifies the narrative your resume tells, and flags where remote-readiness signals are missing. It's the feedback a good mentor would give you, not what a polite chatbot defaults to.

### How to use it

If you use Claude Code: clone the repo, open it, and start with "Help me build my resume." Skills are auto-discovered.

If you use any other AI: browse the `prompts/` folder and copy-paste into your conversation.
