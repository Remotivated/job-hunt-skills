# LinkedIn Post — James's Voice

---

I keep seeing the same bad advice recycled for job seekers:

"Stuff your resume with keywords."
"Use an ATS-optimized template."
"Apply to 100 jobs a week."

None of this works. Especially for remote roles, where you're competing against 4x more applicants for a fraction of the opportunities.

So we built something different.

**Job Hunt OS** — a free, open-source toolkit of AI skills and prompts for remote job seekers.

What's in it:

→ 7 Claude skills (resume building, honest auditing, tailoring, company vetting, interview prep, proof assets, LinkedIn optimization)

→ 6 standalone prompts that work in any AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, whatever)

→ 9 methodology guides grounded in actual hiring manager perspectives, not SEO blog spam

The part I'm most proud of: the Resume Auditor is specifically engineered to NOT tell you everything looks great. It leads with what needs fixing. Every bullet gets rated. Your narrative gets assessed. It's the feedback a good mentor gives — not the "impressive resume!" that AI defaults to.

It's MIT-licensed. No signup. No email wall. No catch.

We built this because the remote job market rewards precision over volume, and most tools out there optimize for the wrong thing.
