# Resume Audit

## When to Use

When you want honest, critical feedback on your resume — not the "looks great!" response most AI gives by default. Works best with a specific job description to evaluate against, but also works for general feedback.

## What You'll Need

- Your resume (paste the full text)
- A job description (optional but recommended — paste the full text)

## The Prompt

```
You are a hiring manager with 30 seconds to scan this resume. Your job is to find reasons to say no — because that's what real screening looks like.

Do NOT lead with praise. Lead with what needs improvement.

MY RESUME:
[paste your resume here]

JOB DESCRIPTION (if available):
[paste the job description here, or write "General audit — no specific role"]

EVALUATE AT TWO LEVELS:

**Bullet Level — Rate EVERY bullet:**
- STRONG: Specific outcome, clear action, demonstrates capability
- NEEDS WORK: Has potential but lacks specificity or outcome
- WEAK: Vague, passive, or describes activities without results

Flag these patterns: "Responsible for...", "Worked on...", "Helped with...", "Assisted in..." — all weak.

**Narrative Level:**
- What story does this resume tell? Summarize in 2-3 sentences.
- Is the angle clear? (Specialist, generalist, career changer, rising talent?)
- Does it make a coherent case, or read as a disconnected list of facts?

THEN PROVIDE:

1. **30-SECOND SCAN** — What a hiring manager notices (and misses) scanning quickly

2. **TOP 5 REWRITES** — For the 5 weakest bullets, show:
   BEFORE: [original]
   AFTER: [improved version following: Action verb + what you did + specific outcome]
   WHY: [what changed]
   IMPORTANT: Do NOT invent metrics. If a number would help, use [ASK: what was the result?] as a placeholder.

3. **REMOTE-READINESS CHECK** — Are there 1-2 bullets showing remote capability? (async work, self-direction, cross-timezone collaboration, documentation, independent delivery). If missing, suggest how to add them based on my experience.

4. **TERMINOLOGY** — If a job description was provided, flag language mismatches and suggest swaps.

5. **WHAT'S MISSING** — What gaps would make a hiring manager hesitate? What would make them want to talk to me?
```

## What to Expect

A structured report with every bullet rated, your 5 weakest bullets rewritten, narrative-level assessment, remote-readiness evaluation, and specific gaps identified. The feedback will be direct — that's the point.

If the AI starts with "Your resume is impressive..." the prompt didn't work. Ask it to "skip the praise and lead with what needs fixing."
