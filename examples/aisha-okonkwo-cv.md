## Personal Statement

Computational biologist with a PhD from Imperial College and three years as a postdoctoral fellow at the Francis Crick Institute, leading a Wellcome Trust-funded project on transcriptional dynamics in early development. Built and maintain a Nextflow single-cell RNA-seq pipeline used by six labs across the Crick (87 GitHub stars, 4 external contributors). Looking to apply this work to therapeutic discovery in a research-driven biotech where the biology is taken as seriously as the modelling.
