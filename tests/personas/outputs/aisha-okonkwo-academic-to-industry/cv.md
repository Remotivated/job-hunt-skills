## Personal Statement

Computational biologist with four years of postdoctoral experience at the Francis Crick Institute, specialising in single-cell and developmental genomics. I lead the computational biology on a Wellcome Trust-funded project studying transcriptional dynamics in early embryonic development, built a Nextflow pipeline for single-cell RNA-seq that is now used by six labs across the Crick, and have published first-author methods work in *Nature Methods*. I am looking to move into industry as a Senior Computational Biologist at a biology-first techbio company where single-cell analysis connects to real therapeutic questions.
