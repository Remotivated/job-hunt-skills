## Workflow

### 1. Gather current state

Ask the user to paste their current:
- Headline
- About section
- Description of their Featured section (what's pinned, if anything)
- Recent activity (what they post, comment on, share)

If web browsing is available, offer to read their LinkedIn profile directly.

### 2. Audit each section

**Headline:**

Evaluate against the "function + focus" framework:
- Bad: "Marketing Manager at Acme Corp" (just title + company)
- Good: "B2B Marketing Leader | Demand Gen & Content Strategy" (function + focus)
- Include function AND focus, not just title and employer
- If job searching: lead with value, not availability ("Open to opportunities" can follow, but shouldn't lead)

Provide 2-3 rewritten options.

**About Section:**

- Should be narrative, not biography
- Structure: Para 1 (what you do + what drives you), Para 2 (how you approach work), Para 3 (what you're looking for — optional)
- First person — more human, more readable
- Should NOT be resume bullets pasted in
- Should NOT open with "I am a results-driven professional..." or similar filler

Provide a complete rewrite.

**Featured Section:**

- What to pin: case studies, articles, presentations, proof assets, projects
- One strong piece is better than an empty section
- Cross-reference with the proof-asset-creator skill if they need to create something
- Order matters: strongest piece first

Provide specific recommendations for what to add.

**Activity:**

- Comment quality > quantity
- "Great post!" doesn't count — add actual insight, a question, or a related experience
- Sharing articles with a sentence about why it matters
- Occasional original posts showing how you think about your field
- Consistency matters more than volume

Provide specific actions for the next week.

### 3. Produce before/after

Output in conversation:
1. **Headline** — 2-3 options with explanation of tradeoffs
2. **About Section** — Complete rewrite, ready to copy-paste
3. **Featured Section** — Strategy (what to add, in what order, what to create if needed)
4. **Activity Plan** — Specific actions for next 7 days (3 comments, 1 share, 1 DM)
5. **Remote-Readiness** — Ensure remote signals appear naturally (async communication, distributed team experience, self-direction)

All rewritten sections should be ready to copy-paste into LinkedIn.
